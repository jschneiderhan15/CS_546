That Time I Overreacted and Bought an iPad

Last semester, when at work at Retro Fitness - I used to be a front desk worker at the local gym - I realized that classes were about to start and I didn't have any notebooks to take any notes with. I usually bought notebooks before each semseter started in order to make sure that I was prepared for classes. However, since we were just about to start going to classes in person again, I wasn't used to needing notebooks! I wasn't sure what to do because I was pretty lazy and thought that going out to buy notebooks would both be a waste of my time, and also a waste of paper. After this realization, I went online to lookup some note taking alternatives.

I knew that some of my friends had iPads to bring to classes to take notes and do homework with, but I never thought to buy my own because I didn't think I'd use it that much. However, I went on to apple.com to check out any deals they had available. To my luck, I found out that there were a lot of different deals for students like me! After a few hours of deliberation, I decided to buy an iPad under a student discount where I got a free pair of Airpods with it - which made buying a gift for my dad that year very easy. The same day I ordered the iPad, I actually went over to Manhattan to an Apple store to pick it up that night!

After getting the iPad, I was looking at what apps I could get to enhance my experience at all. I discovered a lot of different apps that I already had on my phone / laptop - such as Netflix - but I also found other apps that I'd never used before. One in particular is Procreate, which is an app that I use to draw on! I really like drawing and painting, so I'm glad I could find this application to utilize this hobby of mine. In terms of note taking, I also found a few notetaking apps that were free, which was nice, because the iPad definitely was not a cheap purchase just for something to take notes on!

In conclusion, if ever bored at work and think you need an iPad, I personally think one should go for it. While it definitely was a little expensive for a purchase just to take notes on, I'm glad I made the purchase overall because I use my iPad every day for other things, like drawing and watching Netflix. Whether or not this is extremely productive is definitely up in the air, but I use it enough to be satisfied!
